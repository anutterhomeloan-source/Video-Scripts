---

## Hook (Personal)
When I was brand new to this business, I got handed all the clients nobody else wanted. And honestly? It's the best thing that ever happened to me.

## Branding
I'm Jordan Nutter, and I help make mortgages make sense — and here's *why* I do it the way I do.

## Story
When you're new, you don't get the easy deals. The seasoned agents kept those. What they sent me were the files everybody else passed on. The complicated ones. The people who'd already been told no. The ones who didn't have much.

## Heart
And I'll be honest — at the time, I didn't know it was a gift. But those files taught me everything. And more than that — I got to help people who'd been pushed to the side. Over and over.

## Belief
So here's what I believe. It's the whole reason I do this. Everybody's story is worth the same. Everybody's file deserves the same care. Having less money doesn't make you less. Being in a rough spot financially doesn't make you less. And not growing up with this information — because nobody in your family had it to teach you — that does *not* make you any less.

## End Result
That's who I work with. The people who feel like they don't belong in this process. Because you do. You just needed someone willing to sit down and figure it out with you.

## CTA
So if you've ever felt brushed aside — or like you don't know enough to even start — start with me. Link's in my bio. We'll figure it out together.
