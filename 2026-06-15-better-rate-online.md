# I Found A Better Rate Online

- **Type:** Reenactment (2 parts)
- **Status:** Scripted
- **Date approved:** 2026-06-15
- **CTA:** None
- **Image asset:** None

*Jordan plays both roles: **BORROWER** (excited, a little frantic) and **JORDAN** (calm, slightly suspicious LO). Comedic-panic tone. Each part runs 60+ seconds.*

---

## PART 1 — The "amazing" deal

**BORROWER:** *(breathless, phone to ear)* Jordan! Hey — okay, don't be mad.

**JORDAN:** …Why would I be mad.

**BORROWER:** So I couldn't sleep last night, right? So I'm just scrolling. And this lender pops up — and they quoted me a full percent lower than you. A *whole* percent, Jordan!

**JORDAN:** Okay—

**BORROWER:** Do you know what that does to my monthly payment?? I ran it through one of those little calculator things at like 2 a.m. I almost woke up the whole house.

**JORDAN:** *(calm)* That is a big drop. Where'd you find these people again?

**BORROWER:** I don't know, an ad? It had a really nice logo. Very trustworthy blue.

**JORDAN:** Mm. Trustworthy blue. Did they send you anything in writing, or is this just a number on a screen?

**BORROWER:** No, no, they emailed me a whole official document! It's got my rate right at the top — big, bold, beautiful. Honestly, Jordan? I teared up a little.

**JORDAN:** *(slowly)* You cried over a PDF.

**BORROWER:** A little! It's a good rate!

**JORDAN:** Okay. Before you sign your life away to Trustworthy Blue — do me one favor.

**BORROWER:** *(already typing)* I mean… I kind of already started the application—

**JORDAN:** Of course you did. Scroll down. Read me the fees.

**BORROWER:** The… fees?

**JORDAN:** The fees.

**BORROWER:** *(long pause, scrolling)* …Jordan, there are a lot of words down here that I do not recognize.

**JORDAN:** Mhm. Start reading. I'll wait.

---

## PART 2 — Read me the fees

**JORDAN:** Top one. What's it say?

**BORROWER:** Okay, um… "discount points." Two of 'em. That's… *(squints)* …eight thousand dollars?

**JORDAN:** Right. So that word "discount" — that's not them giving you a discount. That's *you* paying eight grand upfront to buy that rate down. That's how they magically got it a percent lower.

**BORROWER:** *(quieter)* …Oh. That feels like the opposite of a discount.

**JORDAN:** It kind of is. Keep going. What else is living down there?

**BORROWER:** Uh — "processing fee." "Underwriting fee." "Application fee." "Document prep fee." There's a — Jordan, why is there a fee just to *lock* the rate? Why do all these fees have little friends??

**JORDAN:** *(nodding)* That's the junk. Now do this — ignore the rate for one second and find the number that says APR. A-P-R.

**BORROWER:** *(scrolling)* …Okay, found it.

**JORDAN:** Read it out loud.

**BORROWER:** *(beat)* …It's higher than yours.

**JORDAN:** Higher than mine. So the rate looked cheaper, but the actual *loan* is more expensive — because they stuffed all that cost into the fees and just made the top number pretty.

**BORROWER:** So the beautiful document… lied to me.

**JORDAN:** The beautiful document left some things out. The rate is the headline. The APR is the truth.

**BORROWER:** *(defeated)* I really liked that blue.

**JORDAN:** I know you did. Send me both estimates — we'll line 'em up side by side, rate *and* total cost, and you pick for real. No 2 a.m. calculators.

**BORROWER:** *(relieved)* Okay. …Can we delete the application I started?

**JORDAN:** *(sigh)* We'll talk about that part later.

---

## WRAP — End Result (on-screen text or VO)
> A lower rate isn't a lower cost. Always compare the APR and the total fees — not just the big number at the top.

---

## CAPTIONS
