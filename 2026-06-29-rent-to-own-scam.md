# Rent-to-Own Scam

- **Type:** Reenactment (2 parts)
- **Status:** Scripted
- **Date approved:** 2026-06-29
- **CTA:** None
- **Image asset:** None

*Jordan plays all roles: **BORROWER** (excited, gullible) and **JORDAN** (calm, suspicious LO). Scam-alert tone. Each part runs 60–90 seconds.*

---

## PART 1 — "No credit needed!"

**BORROWER:** Jordan! Honestly? I don't even need you anymore. No offense.

**JORDAN:** …None taken? What happened.

**BORROWER:** I found a house on Facebook. Rent-to-own. No credit check. No bank. Nothing.

**JORDAN:** Okay…

**BORROWER:** I just send a deposit, and it's basically mine. Move in this weekend.

**JORDAN:** This weekend.

**BORROWER:** This weekend!

**JORDAN:** Who's "they"?

**BORROWER:** The owner. Super nice guy. He's out of state right now, so we can't meet up.

**JORDAN:** Out of state.

**BORROWER:** For work. He said just Zelle him the deposit. He'll mail the keys.

**JORDAN:** He's gonna mail you… the keys.

**BORROWER:** Is that not how it works?

**JORDAN:** How much is the deposit?

**BORROWER:** Three grand. Way less than you wanted me to save!

**JORDAN:** Do not send that money.

**BORROWER:** …What? Why?

**JORDAN:** Pull the listing back up. Right now. Let's look together.

**TEXT OVERLAY (end card):** *Part 2 — the red flags.*

---

## PART 2 — "The red flags"

**JORDAN:** Okay. The "owner" you've never met. Out of state. Wants Zelle.

**BORROWER:** …When you say it like that.

**JORDAN:** Is the price way below everything else around it?

**BORROWER:** …It's a really good price.

**JORDAN:** Of course it is. That's the bait. Search the address.

**BORROWER:** *(typing)* …It's on three other sites. Different name each time.

**JORDAN:** Because he doesn't own it. Nobody does. He found a vacant house and he's collecting deposits.

**BORROWER:** So I almost gave a stranger three thousand dollars.

**JORDAN:** For keys that don't exist. Listen — rent-to-own can be real. But never wire a deposit to someone you can't meet. For a house nobody'll let you walk through.

**BORROWER:** What do I do instead?

**JORDAN:** You call me. We find a real path. It's not as fast as "this weekend." But it's actually yours.

**BORROWER:** …Okay. I take back the "I don't need you" thing.

**JORDAN:** Yeah. I figured.

---

## PEACE
- **Problem:** Falling for a too-good-to-be-true "no credit needed" rent-to-own listing.
- **Empathy:** It feels like a shortcut around the hard part of buying.
- **Answer:** Red flags — owner you can't meet, out of state, Zelle/wire deposit, price too low, listing duplicated elsewhere.
- **Change:** Never send money for a home you can't walk through or an "owner" you can't verify; call a real lender.
- **End Result:** A legit path to ownership that's actually yours.
