# The Credit Dispute "Boost"

- **Type:** Reenactment (2 parts)
- **Status:** Scripted
- **Date approved:** 2026-07-07
- **CTA:** None
- **Image asset:** None

*Jordan plays all roles: **BORROWER** (proud, then deflated) and **JORDAN** (calm, dry LO). Comedic tone. The "credit repair guy" is referenced, not a character. Each part runs 60–90 seconds.*

**Lesson:** Disputing derogatory accounts just flags them "in dispute" and temporarily hides them from the score. To close a loan those disputes must be resolved/removed — the accounts come back, the "boost" disappears, and active disputes can stall the loan entirely.

---

## PART 1 — "I fixed my credit!"

**BORROWER:** Jordan, cancel the credit talk. Handled it.

**JORDAN:** …Handled it.

**BORROWER:** My score jumped 90 points in three weeks. You're welcome.

**JORDAN:** Ninety points. In three weeks. *How.*

**BORROWER:** I found this credit repair guy. Saw his ad, gave him a call. He fixed the whole thing.

**JORDAN:** A credit repair guy. From an ad.

**BORROWER:** Five stars, Jordan. He disputed everything. Every negative thing on my report — gone. Poof.

**JORDAN:** He disputed… everything.

**BORROWER:** Everything! The late payments, the old collection, that medical bill. Just marked it all "this isn't mine."

**JORDAN:** But… some of that *was* yours.

**BORROWER:** Details. The score went up! That's what matters, right?

**JORDAN:** Mmm. Pull your report back up for me.

**BORROWER:** Why? It's beautiful now.

**JORDAN:** Humor me. Look at those accounts. What's it say next to them?

**BORROWER:** *(squinting)* …There's a little note. "Disputed by consumer."

**JORDAN:** Yep. And that little note? That's about to be a really big problem.

**BORROWER:** …A problem? It literally says I WIN.

**JORDAN:** Sit tight.

**TEXT OVERLAY (end card):** *Part 2 — why "disputed" blocks your loan.*

---

## PART 2 — "Why 'disputed' blocks your loan"

**JORDAN:** Okay. Here's what you didn't realize.

**BORROWER:** Uh oh. You've got the voice.

**JORDAN:** When that guy disputed your derogatory accounts, they didn't disappear. They just got flagged "in dispute." And your score ignores them for a minute. That's your fake 90 points.

**BORROWER:** …Fake?

**JORDAN:** To close your loan, those disputes have to come *off*. And when they do, the accounts come back. Real, negative, right back on your report.

**BORROWER:** So my beautiful score…

**JORDAN:** Goes right back where it started. And here's the kicker — we can't even move forward on your loan until every one of those disputes is resolved.

**BORROWER:** So I didn't fix my credit. I paused it. And made it worse.

**JORDAN:** You paused it. And added a few weeks of cleanup.

**BORROWER:** …Can we un-dispute them?

**JORDAN:** We can. And then we fix it the *real* way — the way I offered you for free. Before you paid a guy to make it worse.

**BORROWER:** I don't wanna talk about the guy.

**JORDAN:** I know you don't.

---

## PEACE
- **Problem:** Paying a credit-repair service to dispute every derogatory account for a fast "boost."
- **Empathy:** A quick score jump feels like the shortcut to qualifying.
- **Answer:** Disputes just flag accounts and temporarily hide them; they must be resolved to close, and the accounts (and score drop) come back.
- **Change:** Fix credit the real way — actually work through the accounts; don't pay someone to dispute valid debts.
- **End Result:** A real, loan-ready score instead of a fake one that stalls the deal.
