# Co-Signed Secret

- **Type:** Reenactment (2 parts)
- **Status:** Scripted
- **Date approved:** 2026-06-22
- **CTA:** None
- **Image asset:** None

*Jordan plays all roles: **JORDAN** (calm LO), **SARAH** (wife), **MIKE** (husband, fumbling). Play the reveal straight — the awkward silence does the work; the comedy lands in Part 2 with Jordan calm and Mike spiraling. Each part runs 60–90 seconds.*

---

## PART 1 — The Confirmation Call

*Staging: Jordan at her desk, couple on speaker. Quick switches for Mike & Sarah.*

**JORDAN:** Alright, you two are basically done — I just need to confirm the debts that came up on your credit so the application matches exactly. Cool if I run through 'em real quick?

**SARAH:** Yeah, go for it.

**JORDAN:** Okay. I've got the mortgage on your current place, a Visa, a Discover card, and the auto loan on the Nissan. That all sound right?

**SARAH:** Yep — that's our car. That's everything.

**JORDAN:** Perfect. And then the *second* auto loan—

**SARAH:** …Wait. The second one? We only have the Nissan.

**JORDAN:** Hmm, let me look. *(pause)* Yeah — there's a second auto loan here. It's co-signed. It's got Mike's name on it.

**SARAH:** Mike's name? Babe… did you co-sign for somebody?

**MIKE:** I— uh— it's not a big deal, a friend needed a co-signer for a car, that's all.

**SARAH:** A *friend.* *(slowly)* …What friend, Mike? Who would you co-sign a whole *car* for?

**MIKE:** It's just— it's somebody from— it's not what you're—

**SARAH:** *(it clicks — points)* …Is it her? Mike. Is it HER?

**MIKE:** Okay can we— can we not do this right now—

**JORDAN (to camera, reaching for mute):** …I'm gonna give you two a minute.

**TEXT OVERLAY (end card):** *Part 2: then he called me back FURIOUS.*

---

## PART 2 — He Called Me Back

*Staging: Jordan answers a second call. Mike only — heated.*

**MIKE:** Jordan, I gotta be honest — you had no business bringing that loan up in front of my wife.

**JORDAN:** …The co-signed car?

**MIKE:** Yes! You aired out my personal business in two seconds flat.

**JORDAN:** Mike, I confirm liabilities with every single borrower — it's part of the application. That loan is sitting on your credit report. I didn't put it there. I just read what was on my screen.

**MIKE:** You didn't have to say it out loud while she was sitting *right there!*

**JORDAN:** Okay, here's what I actually need you to hear. Even if I'd never said one word about it? The second you two go under contract, every debt you have is printed in the loan documents. The ones you *both* sign. At the closing table.

**MIKE:** …

**JORDAN:** So she wasn't finding out because of me. She was *always* going to find out. I just happened to be holding the report.

**MIKE:** *(quiet)* …So what am I supposed to tell her?

**JORDAN:** *(beat)* Mike… that part's above my pay grade. I just do the mortgage.

---

## PEACE
- **Problem:** Thinking you can hide a debt / a co-sign from your spouse or your lender.
- **Empathy:** Most people don't realize a co-signed loan even shows up.
- **Answer:** Liabilities live on your credit report AND in the loan documents you both sign.
- **Change:** Be upfront with your lender (and your spouse) from the start.
- **End Result:** No ambushes at the closing table.
